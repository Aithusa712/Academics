The program can:
- Read .txt files
- Insert customer information from .txt files into a data structure
- Calculate change amount into coin denomination for every customer
- Handle user input.
- Find and display Customer and their information.
- Generate/update .csv files with customer information


The program cannot:
- Accept characters other than 0-9 or else it will go into an infinite loop.
- Accept customer information with different phrasing or format.
